Hi there, I'm Britnay Beaudry.

I'm currently the Geoinformatics Fellow for the NASA DEVELOP National Program at Ames Research Center. At DEVELOP, I have the amazing opportunity to work with teams on 10-week feasibility studies that use NASA Earth Observations to address environmental issues to community concerns around the globe. My personal research interests surround aquatic remote sensing, environmental data science, and machine learning.

When I'm not working, I'm usually spending time with family, attempting a new vegetarian recipe, or trying out new things related to science and tech (which is how this site got created in the first place). I love thin crust pizza, I try to read a new book each month, and my happy place is probably Trader Joe's.

If I haven't completely scared you off, you are more than welcome to read more about me and my work here.
