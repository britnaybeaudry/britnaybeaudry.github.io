Hi there, I'm Britnay Beaudry.

I'm currently the Geoinformatics Fellow for the NASA DEVELOP National Program at Ames Research Center. At DEVELOP, I have the amazing opportunity to work with teams on 10-week feasibility studies that address environmental issues to community concerns around the globe, by applying the lens of NASA Earth observations. My personal research interests surround aquatic remote sensing, environmental data science, and machine learning.
